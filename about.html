Our Story

How We Started

Founded over a decade ago, Mudanzas Edyta London began as a small family business with a simple mission: to make moving stress-free for everyone in London. What started with just one van and a passion for helping people has grown into one of London's most trusted removal services.

Where it all began - our first van in 2014

The Personal Touch

Our founder, Edyta, moved to London herself and experienced firsthand the challenges of relocating in a busy city. She understood the importance of having reliable, professional help during one of life's most stressful events. This personal experience became the foundation of our company's commitment to exceptional service.
