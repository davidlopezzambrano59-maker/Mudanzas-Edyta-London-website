Moving day is won or lost at the kerb. In Morden, Controlled Parking Zones are not decorative—they decide where a van can sit, for how long, and whether you are loading legally or creating a problem that slows the whole job.

M3 runs 8:30am–6:30pm Monday to Sunday and demand is especially tight near Morden Station, the Northern Line terminus where congestion spikes and legal stopping space disappears fast.
